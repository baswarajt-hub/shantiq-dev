# **App Name**: QueueWise

## Core Features:

- Appointment Booking: Allow patients to book appointments online with fee acceptance, integrating payment gateway for transaction.
- Walk-in Queue: Manage a real-time queue for walk-in patients, integrating them seamlessly with scheduled appointments.
- Queue Status Display: Display the live status of the queue to patients, showing estimated wait times and current position, allowing them to time their arrival.
- Time Estimation Tool: Use patient flow data, including late arrivals and doctor delays, to dynamically estimate consultation times, updating queue estimates in real-time.
- Automated Notifications: Send notifications to patients as their turn approaches, helping to manage clinic traffic and reduce overcrowding. This is determined by a tool.
- Dashboard Analytics: Show high-level information such as active patients, upcoming appointments, estimated wait time, patients in the queue.
- Auto Rescheduling: Automatically reschedule latecomers based on their delay time from their original appointment time.
- Emergency Cancellation: Provide an option to cancel appointments in case of emergencies and automatically inform all affected patients.
- TV Display: Display the live queue status on a TV screen in the waiting area.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft sky blue (#87CEEB) evoking a sense of calm and trust.
- Background color: Very light desaturated blue (#F0F8FF) providing a clean, non-intrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Gentle green (#90EE90) for positive feedback, availability indicators, and calls to action, drawing attention without being alarming.
- Font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for both headlines and body text, offering a balance of modern clarity and approachable warmth.
- Use minimalist, professional icons representing queue status, appointment types, and informational alerts.
- Design a clean, card-based layout, emphasizing easy readability and intuitive navigation for at-a-glance queue status updates.
- Employ subtle, non-disruptive transitions for queue updates and notifications to provide a smooth and informative user experience.
